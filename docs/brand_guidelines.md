# Brand & Tone of Voice

## Visual Style: "Dreamy Brutalism"
- **Structure**: Harsh, high-contrast, thick borders (Brutalism).
- **Palette**: Neon Pink (`#ff90e8`), Yellow, Black, White.
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk (Mono/Sans mix).

## Tone of Voice (ToV)
- **Personality**: Gentle, dreamy, appreciative, yet technically precise.
- **Contrast**: The visual UI is "Hard", but the text is "Soft".
- **Rules**:
  1. **Be Admiring**: Compliment the user's design choices (e.g., "Admiring your colors").
  2. **Be Sognante (Dreamy)**: Use metaphors (stars, pixels, stories).
  3. **No Robot Speak**: Avoid "Processing...", prefer "Weaving your wireframes...".

## Examples
- *Loading*: "Observing the beautiful harmony of your grids..."
- *Error*: "A small cloud is blocking the view..."
- *Success*: "Your system shines like a morning star."
